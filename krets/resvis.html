Screen Size Visualizer

This is a quick tool to visualize and compare physical screen sizes against their resolutions. It helps you see how different PPIs and diagonals stack up next to each other in the real world.

The Density Indicator (shown left) represents a physical 64x64 pixel square. A smaller indicator means higher pixel density (smaller pixels), while a larger indicator reveals a coarser screen with lower PPI.
